Since inception in 2017, Tadem Reliable Services (TRS) has been focusing on providing engineering solutions and construction services. We have completed many successful projects that include residential, commercial, infra and industrial projects. Finest precision can be seen in every work of us.

We execute one single contract to provide all architectural or engineering design services, scheduling activities, resources’ procurement, construction, installation and commissioning etc., under one umbrella. Our engineering solutions generally include Architecture, Structural, Electrical, Mechanical, HVAC, Plumbing and Sewerage, Fire Protection services, Building Management systems, and Infrastructure works, Procurement Services, Project Management and Construction services.

We never neglect our sacred duty of giving value to the money invested by our customers. We have gained expertise to deliver end-to-end solutions and services catering to engineering, procurement and construction in the field of residential and commercial buildings, infrastructure and industrial projects. While delivering world-class solutions and services to our customers, we follow all environmental, health, safety and quality standards through a process driven approach.

We undertake specific responsibility for proper coordination amongst various contractors and subcontractors connected with the projects. Exceptional execution is the key to create projects on time. We leverage technology, advanced construction methods and well-arranged collaboration between diverse specialists to ensure flawless and on time execution of projects. Working with integrity, we ensure that all our solutions and services are infused with the highest standard of quality and safety.

Vision

Shall be a most respected, admired and leading global enterprise in world-class engineering and construction services through sustainable growth.

Mission

To create new benchmarks in engineering and construction services with a commitment to maintain the highest quality standards and executing all projects on time.

Our team combines professionalism, experience and expertise to turn dreams into reality. We have qualified professional engineers, managers, supervisors and other technicians. Over and above these, we have good professional relationship with all leading consultants (Architects, Structural and Service Consultants etc.) across the globe. We may seek their services as and when required in the best interest of the projects.

The key factors that guide and envision this dedicated team are reliability, stability and a focused outlook. Working in tandem with each other, right from sourcing of material to execution of every project, the team takes pride in what they do and how they achieve their goals within a set time frame with quality at every stage being the benchmark of success. Our commitment to quality and innovation stems from its rich experience and expertise. We ensure that only the brightest talent that is focused on the task at hand is retained across all functions.
